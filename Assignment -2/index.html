About Company

Jay Crockett, commonly referred to as Trew in the entertainment industry, is an experienced musician, songwriter, and voiceover actor inspired by the likes of Michael Jackson, Stevie Wonder, Camp Lo, and Sly & The Family Stone.

Recognized for his innovative approach to music, he regularly insists on pushing the limits, breaking down barriers, and thinking outside the box while creating music throughout various genres.

Our Services

Music Production and Creation

With a team of talented producers, engineers, and musicians, Clocked Out is a hub for music creation that transcends genres and pushes the boundaries of sonic innovation. We believe in nurturing the seed of every artistic idea and transforming it into a masterpiece.

A&R and Artist Development

Our dedication to nurturing emerging talent is unwavering. As A&R specialists, we carefully select and mentor promising artists, helping them grow both artistically and professionally, empowering them to forge successful careers in the industry.

Music for Media

Clocked Out has earned a prestigious reputation for crafting exceptional music for television shows, movies, and various brand and ad campaigns. Our collaboration with renowned music supervisors and marketing firms has led to placements with top-tier networks like MTV, VH1, Sony, Warner Bros, and many others.

Opening for Legends

We take pride in providing opportunities for aspiring artists to share the stage with music legends. Our history includes opening acts for iconic artists like De La Soul, Devo, Jacks Mannequin, and more. An experience that leaves a lasting impact on every artist we work with.
